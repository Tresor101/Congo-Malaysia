About Our Community

Connecting Congolese hearts across Malaysia, celebrating our rich heritage, and building a supportive network for all community members.

Our Story

The Congolese Community in Malaysia is a vibrant network of individuals from the Democratic Republic of Congo living, working, and studying across Malaysia. We are united by our shared heritage and commitment to supporting one another.

Since our establishment, we've been dedicated to preserving our cultural identity while embracing the diversity of Malaysian society. We organize cultural events, provide mutual support, and create opportunities for our members to connect and thrive.
