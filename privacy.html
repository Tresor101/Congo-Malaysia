Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

The Congolese Community in Malaysia is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data collection and privacy when you visit our website.

Our Privacy Commitment

We respect your privacy completely. We do not collect, store, track, or share any personal data from visitors to our website. You can browse our site with complete peace of mind knowing that your privacy is fully protected.

1 Information We DO NOT Collect

Our website is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be transparent about what we do not collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your browsing behavior
  • No Analytics Data: We do not collect information about which pages you visit, how long you stay, or how you navigate our site
  • No Technical Data: We do not collect IP addresses, browser types, device information, or operating system details
  • No Location Data: We do not track or collect your geographic location
  • No Social Media Tracking: We do not use social media pixels or tracking codes
100% Privacy Guaranteed: This is a simple, static website designed to share information about our community. We have no forms, no sign-ups, and no data collection mechanisms.

2 How You Can Browse Safely

Your visit to our website is completely anonymous and private:

  • Browse any page without worrying about being tracked
  • No need to accept cookie banners or privacy notices
  • View photos, read about events, and explore our community freely
  • Your information stays with you - we don't collect anything

3 External Links and Third-Party Content

While we don't collect any data, please be aware of the following:

  • WhatsApp Links: When you click on our WhatsApp community group link, you leave our website and enter WhatsApp's platform, which has its own privacy policy
  • Instagram Links: Clicking on Instagram links will take you to Instagram's platform, governed by Meta's privacy policies
  • Google Drive/Photos: Event photos hosted on Google services are subject to Google's privacy terms
  • 500px Portfolio: Photography portfolios on 500px are governed by their privacy policy
Important: We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites and services. Please review their individual privacy policies before sharing any information with them.

4 Web Hosting

Our website is hosted on a standard web hosting service. While we don't collect data, your web hosting provider may collect basic server logs for technical purposes. This is standard practice for all websites and is beyond our control. These logs typically include:

  • IP addresses for security and anti-abuse purposes
  • Basic access logs required for server operation
  • Technical error logs for troubleshooting

These logs are managed by the hosting provider and are not accessible to us for data analysis or marketing purposes.

5 Photography and Event Images

Our website displays photos from community events:

  • Photos are publicly viewable on our website and social media
  • We respect the privacy of individuals in photos
  • If you appear in a photo and wish to have it removed, please contact us
  • We will promptly remove or blur any photos upon request
  • No facial recognition or identification technology is used

6 Contact and Communication

If you choose to contact us through WhatsApp or Instagram:

  • Communication happens directly on those platforms
  • We only use the information you voluntarily share to respond to your inquiry
  • We do not store contact information in any database
  • Your messages are handled with respect and confidentiality
  • We do not share your contact details with third parties

7 Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever decide to collect any data in the future (which is not currently planned), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy with clear information about what data is collected and why
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Announce any significant changes to the community
  • Always maintain transparency about our data practices

8 Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, you don't need to worry about:

  • Requesting data deletion (there's nothing to delete)
  • Requesting data access (there's nothing to access)
  • Opting out of marketing (we don't do marketing)
  • Managing consent preferences (no consent needed)
Simple Privacy: The best way to protect your privacy is to not collect data in the first place. That's our approach!

9 Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any data from anyone, we also don't collect data from children. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected when viewing our site.

10 Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Malaysia. Our commitment to not collecting data exceeds the minimum requirements of most privacy regulations worldwide.
